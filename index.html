SOLSTICE

The Sun Remembers

You are Sol — the very soul of the Sun itself. Not a human being. You are the light that has illuminated the Earth and sustained all life for billions of years.

But now you have forgotten why you rise. Five ancient memories have been lost. Without them, your light slowly fades. The seasons stop. The world grows dark.

Explore this world, recover your lost memories, and answer each challenge before your light runs out forever.

HOW TO PLAY

🏆
HOW TO WIN

Restore all 5 of Sol's lost memories by reaching each chapter's objective and solving its puzzle. Complete all chapters before Sol's light fades.

🌑
HOW TO LOSE

Sol's Light (the golden bar at the top) drains over time. If it reaches zero before you restore all memories, eternal darkness falls.
